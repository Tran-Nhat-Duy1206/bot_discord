Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Slime Bot ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Slime Bot Discord application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our bot service integrated with Google OAuth.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize Slime Bot to access your Google account, we may collect:

  • Google Account Information: Your email address and basic profile information from your Google account.
  • Google Drive Access: When you use deadline features, the bot creates and manages Google Sheets/Documents on your behalf within your personal Google Drive.
  • Discord Data: Server ID, channel ID, and message content necessary for bot functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide deadline management services (creating and updating Google Sheets)
  • Enable Google Calendar integration for deadline reminders
  • Improve and maintain bot functionality

4. Data Storage

Your OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely. Google Sheets and Documents created by the bot are stored in YOUR personal Google Drive account, not on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your Google data remains under your control and in your Google Drive.

6. Google OAuth

Our bot uses Google OAuth 2.0 for user authentication. When you authorize our application:

  • You grant us limited access to your Google account as specified in the OAuth consent screen
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • We only access data necessary for the bot's functionality

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Google OAuth access at any time via Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of your data stored by the bot
  • Request access to information we hold about you
